Our History

SuperCars Co., Ltd, Lesotho Multinational Corporation, known as a distributor of luxury, high performance Automobiles and Motorcycles, was found on 24th September 2022 by Dr. Motumi Katali, Mr. Letsekang Matubatuba, Sir. Nqhae Qhobela, Prof. Patrick Matlali, and Sebuti Mofokeng.


Aims And Objectives

  • Our objectives is to be recognised as one of the world's most expensive car traders.

  • To win best seller award in 5 years.

  • To find and supply the most exclusive cars for high earners and royal clans.

  • To have an international franchise

Organizational Structure

  • Founder(s)-Prof. Matlali: Founder and Owner.

  • CEO- Dr. Motumi Katali: Overlooks the entire company activities in all locations.

  • MANAGER-Mr. Letsekang Matubatuba: Manages staff.

  • ACCOUNTANT- Sir Nqhae Qhobela: Incharge of record keeping.

  • SALES CLERK- Sebuti Mofokeng: A professional sales consultant.
